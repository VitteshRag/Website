About Me

Hello! I'm Vittesh Raghavan, a passionate game designer with a love for narrative-driven experiences and immersive worlds. My journey in game design began with a fascination for how stories and mechanics intertwine to create memorable moments for players. Over the years, I've honed my skills in level design, systems design, and interactive storytelling, always striving to craft gameplay loops that are both engaging and meaningful.

I believe that great games are built on a foundation of thoughtful design, player empathy, and a willingness to experiment. Whether working solo or collaborating with a team, I approach every project with curiosity and a drive to push creative boundaries. My portfolio reflects a diverse range of projects, from atmospheric horror to cooperative puzzles, each one a testament to my dedication and growth as a designer.

Outside of game development, I enjoy exploring new genres, analyzing game mechanics, and sharing insights with fellow designers. I'm always eager to learn, adapt, and contribute to the ever-evolving world of interactive entertainment.
